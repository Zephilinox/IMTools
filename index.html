Tool Shop

I M Tools is a dedicated tool shop based in Burnham on Sea, Somerset. With a huge range of power tools, hand tools, safety wear, gloves, goggles, blades and equipment for painting and decorating and much more!

Pop in and ask if you are looking for something in particular. With next day turnaround on orders there shouldn't be any delay.

The shop is open from 7 am making it convenient for local trades people to stock up on their tools. With competitive pricing many items are cheaper than national stores.

Starting a DIY project? If you need advice on the best tools and equipment to use then I M Tools is the perfect place to visit. From small spirit levels, hammers, saws and ear defenders to drills, jigsaws and other power tools - just ask if you can't find what you need!

We stock many well know brands such as Makita, Hitachi, Stanley, Refina, Estwing, Milwaukee and Marshalltown.
